About me

I am a seasoned graphic designer with over 20 years of experience in creating compelling visual stories that resonate with audiences across digital and print platforms. My expertise spans brand identity development, creative direction, and design strategy, with a focus on bringing brands to life through innovative and consistent visual communication.

Throughout my career, I’ve collaborated with diverse teams across various industries, delivering high-quality design solutions that enhance brand presence and drive results. From designing logos and marketing materials to managing large-scale design projects, I thrive on transforming complex ideas into visually engaging and effective designs. I am passionate about design that not only looks beautiful but also serves a purpose—whether it’s improving brand recognition, engaging audiences, or telling a story. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of design principles, I take pride in delivering work that is both aesthetically pleasing and strategically aligned with my clients’ goals.

Good design is not just what looks good. It also needs to perform, convert, astonish, and fulfill its purpose. The majority of today’s consumers come to a business through social media and other web based platforms where attention is drawn by aesthetic elements.

With a diverse background and over 20 years of experience in graphic and web design, I have created innovative, intuitive, and functional designs to fulfill the needs of a wide range of organizations. Whether it be a break-through product or a simple redesign, I am your person.
